Welcome to ThreeDaFace 

This demo is an ongoing effort, developed by Amir Tal and Avi Haimov under the supervision of Tal Hassner, as part of an undergraduate final project at the Academic College of Tel-Aviv Yaffo.


The algorithms used here are based on the ones described in the paper:
Tal Hassner and Ronen Basri, Example Based 3D Reconstruction from Single 2D Images, Beyond Patches Workshop at IEEE Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR), New-York, June 2006 with some unpublished modifications. If you use this demo for your own work, please cite this paper.
